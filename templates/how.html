    • HOW IT WORKS

    Readitate matches books with particular emotions. Feeling a little lonely? There's a book for that. Started a new relationship recently? There's a book for that, too. The idea is to make the reader feel understood, like they are a part of something far more universal. You might ask, "What do I do?" Easy. All you have to do is type in how you're feeling.
